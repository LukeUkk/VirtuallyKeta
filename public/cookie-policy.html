What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device. They help websites work properly and provide information about how the site is used.

Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: required for basic site functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand traffic and improve the site (for example, Google Analytics).
  • Third-party cookies: may be set if you visit external services such as scheduling or social platforms.

How To Manage Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. You can also use privacy-focused browser tools to limit tracking. If you disable cookies, some parts of the website may not work as intended.
