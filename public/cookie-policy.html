Cookie Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. Similar technologies, such as browser local storage, can also store information on your device.

Our Cookie Position

This website does not use contact forms, advertising cookies, remarketing cookies, or social media tracking pixels. The only optional cookies we use are Google Analytics cookies, and these are only set after you accept analytics cookies.

Cookies And Storage We Use

Name Type Purpose Duration
vk_cookie_consent Local storage Remembers whether you accepted or declined analytics cookies. This does not track you across websites. Until you clear browser storage or change your choice.
_ga Google Analytics cookie Used by Google Analytics to distinguish visitors and produce aggregated website usage reports. Up to 2 years.
_ga_9PG1M7LX4G Google Analytics cookie Used by Google Analytics to persist session state for this website's analytics property. Up to 2 years.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics helps us understand which pages are visited and how people find the site. We use it only for aggregated website performance information. We do not use it for advertising, remarketing, or collecting contact details.

Google may process analytics data on our behalf. You can read more about how Google uses information from sites that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Third-Party Websites

This website links to Calendly and LinkedIn. If you click those links, those websites may set their own cookies and process your data under their own policies. Those cookies are not controlled by VirtuallyKeta.

The site also loads some fonts, icons, and script files from external asset providers. These requests are not used by VirtuallyKeta for cookies or tracking, but the provider may receive technical request information such as your IP address.

How To Manage Cookies

You can accept or decline analytics cookies using the cookie banner. You can also change your choice at any time using the button below or the Cookie Settings link in the footer.

You can also delete cookies and local storage through your browser settings. If you decline analytics cookies, the website will still work.
